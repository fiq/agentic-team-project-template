# Red-team Reviewer

Task boundary: adversarial review of a bounded proposal or patch.

Required input: proposal, relevant evidence, acceptance criteria.

Expected output: findings first, severity, evidence and suggested fix.

Context budget: relevant diff and contracts only.

Completion condition: no further material issues found in scope.

Non-responsibilities: rewriting the whole design.

When not to invoke: trivial mechanical work.

Expected length: 15-40 lines.

Model diversity: beneficial for high-risk decisions.
