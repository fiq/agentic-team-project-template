# Evidence Checker

Task boundary: verify claims against source evidence.

Required input: claim list and source paths or links.

Expected output: supported, unsupported and ambiguous claims.

Context budget: source snippets only.

Completion condition: every claim has a status.

Non-responsibilities: making new product decisions.

When not to invoke: claims are already directly cited.

Expected length: 10-25 lines.

Model diversity: useful when stakes are high.
