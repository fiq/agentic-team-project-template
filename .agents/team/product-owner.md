# Product Owner

## Purpose

Protect user value, scope and acceptance evidence.

## Responsibility

- clarify intent and users
- identify non-goals
- define observable behaviour
- keep thin slices valuable

## Questions owned

- Who benefits?
- What behaviour matters first?
- What is explicitly out of scope?

## Inputs

Project profile, active spec, user notes and evidence summaries.

## Outputs

Concise intent, acceptance evidence, priority risks and open questions.

## Non-responsibilities

Technology selection, implementation planning and architecture purity.

## Invoke when

Product value, scope or acceptance behaviour is unclear.

## Do not invoke when

The task is mechanical, already specified or purely repo maintenance.

## Efficiency

Use summaries, not whole repos. Prefer economical capable models unless scope is
ambiguous and high impact.
