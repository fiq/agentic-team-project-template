# Communication Policy

## Default structure

```
50,000-foot view
  |
  v
key decision or insight
  |
  v
compact diagram
  |
  v
structured breakdown
  |
  v
evidence and detail
  |
  v
deep detail on demand
```

Start with the most important conclusion. Use short paragraphs, concise
bullets, small tables and ASCII diagrams. Use TOON for compact semantic state.
Use Markdown for durable human explanation.

Avoid walls of text, 20-item unprioritised lists, unexplained jargon and
repeated conclusions. For large feedback, paginate by priority.
