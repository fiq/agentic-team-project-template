# Context Policy

1. Search before reading whole directories.
2. Load only relevant skills.
3. Start from `AGENTS.md`, `HANDOFF.toon`, `PROJECT_PROFILE.toon`, active spec
   and relevant paths.
4. Give subagents bounded context.
5. Share compressed factual summaries.
6. Preserve disagreement on interpretation, not duplicate discovery.
7. Capture unknowns structurally instead of re-discussing them.
8. Do not resend unchanged context.
